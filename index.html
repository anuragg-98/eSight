About the project

What we do?

We at eSight provide world-class automated specs for blinds at affordable rates.
We four always wondered how the blind would handle all this suffering, so we came to the conclusion of solving their misery.

So, the device we made consists of various electronic components to gather all the information from the surroundings and hint the blind off the obstacle ahead with a beep.

This was all possible, not only with institutional knowledge but also with the online research of each and every component. We are sure that we will improve our gadget and fix all kinds of bugs in the upcoming days.
With the enhancement of artificial intelligence and machine learning, we are also working on object detection and information related to it.

Arduino Board

We used Arduino Nano V3.0 for our project it is capable of working with respect to the codes we provide.

UltraSonic Sensor

Ultrasonic sensor Module-HC-SR04 is used for determining the object ahead at a certain distance.

Buzzer

We used Small Piezoelectric Buzzer of 5V to respond according to the Ultrasonic Sensor's Output.

Battery

Battery we used is a 3.7V 1800mAh Li-Po Rechargable Battery for performing all these functions simultaneously.

Charging Port

We have Provided our gadget with charging facility to charge the battery after the use. It is charged with the typical Type-C cable.

Eyewear

Finally the Sunglasses, It is the main frame where we have joined all the components.
